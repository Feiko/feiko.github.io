About Feiko Gorter

Hi I’m Feiko Gorter, husband, father, Internet of Things specialist and .net developer. Born in 1985, the youngest of four brothers, we already owned or own computer more powerful than the computer used to land on the moon. Today most of the billions of IoT devices are a multitude faster than our old computer. Like my brothers I became fascinated by these computers. I was so much fascinated by computers that after a career detour I decided to go back into the fascinating world of computers, specially the Internet of Things.

Being an early adopter of IoT technology, I’ve seen the world of IoT evolve. There is so much going on that it’s hard to keep track specially if your new to this domain. Therefore I decided to create this blog and vlog website to share my humble knowledge with world. But mostly also to have fun and share my personal IT adventures.
